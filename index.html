What is SnowflakeBaby.com?

SnowflakeBaby.com is a self-matching service that connects families pursuing embryo adoption to potential donors who have struggled with their own fertility issues and want to donate their unused embryos to families who need them.

Who Benefits?

Embryo Donors

Potential embryo donors are stuck with the moral and ethical dilemma of how to handle unused embryos while continuing to cover the expense of long term storage fees.

Embryo Recipients

Potential recipients may be excluded from an opportunities to have their own families due to high cost of fertility treatments and traditional adoption and physical limitations that prevent natural conception.

Cryopreservation Facilities

Cryopreservation and storage facilities are exposed to legal implications of high abandonment rates and having to determine how to handle abandoned embryos.

Today there are an estimated 600,000 to 4 million frozen embryos stored in the United States that are abandoned or unclaimed, representing an enormous burden for clinics and storage facilities. "...reports indicate some owners of unused embryos cut off contact with clinics or storage facilities out of guilt: They want neither to have additional children nor to continue paying storage fees, but they don't want to make the call to have the embryos destroyed. "

Vaughn, Richard. “What to Do with Abandoned Embryos.” IFLG - International Fertility Law Group, 7 Nov. 2017, www.iflg.net/abandoned-embryos/. Accessed 24 May 2021.

How Does it Work?

Sign Up and Create a Profile

Create a profile and sign up to access our embryo donor recipient database.

Search Profiles to Find a Match

Use search and filter features to find potential matches to build your family unit.

Contact Your Potential Donor/Recipient

Communicate with potential matches through our messaging system until you are comfortable with direct contact with your selected match.

Agree to Terms and Make Arrangements to Transfer/Recieve Embryos

ie. transfer of storage fees, adoption agreement type, etc. Have a legal representative review documentation and make arrangements to complete the transfer to a local facility until use.

Why Choose Us?

Cost

$25/mth to find a match for recipients (free for donors) as opposed to $2500 in non profit program fees, or ten of thousands in traditional adoption agency fees, or up to hundreds of thousands for IVF treatments.

Time

Donors & Recipients can move forward with the ‘property transfer’ as quickly, or slowly as they would like, they are not held back by agency timelines and lengthy legal processes.

Access

Donors & Recipients can choose the level of legal involvement and contractual agreements they are comfortable with. Expensive and invasive home studies and agency interference not required.
